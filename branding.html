Graphic Profile for NREC


We encourage all our users to use, share and promote our logo. To use the logo, make sure the logo has appropriate contrast with the background. It is best to use the version of the logo with the most contrast. If the background is too busy, it is recommended to blur the background.

Logo Usage Guidelines

    DOs:
  • Use appropriate margin around the logo to let it breathe
  • The Cloud can be used by itself
  • Make sure that the logo has appropriate contrast with the background
    DON'Ts:
  • Do not use colors other than the main colors
  • Do not adjust or crop the letters or the logo
  • Do not add effect to the logo
  • Do not extend the logo (e.g. NREC/UH-IaaS)


If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact us.
